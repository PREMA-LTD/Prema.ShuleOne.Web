Manual Payment Process

  1. Go to 'Lipa na Mpesa'

    Navigate to the 'Lipa na Mpesa' option on your phone.

  2. Select 'Pay Bill'

    Choose the 'Pay Bill' option from the menu.

  3. Enter business number '4148251'

    Type the business number as '4148251' in the required field.

  4. Enter account number

    Provide the account number as 'admission'.

  5. Enter fees amount

    Input 200.

  6. Enter your Mpesa PIN

    Enter your secret Mpesa PIN to authorize the transaction.

  7. Confirm transaction

    Review the details and confirm the payment.

  8. Receive SMS confirmation

    You will receive an SMS confirming the payment was successful.
